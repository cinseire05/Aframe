Developing Arts in kids

This website helps teach kids express themselves through many different kinds arts. DAK gives your kid daily assignments, that tells them to draw whats on their minds or how they are feel that day through various drawing challenges. Like on one day it could say what you seen today or draw a picture that explain how your day is going or went. Art can express how you feel and can determine what your thinking about and it can just be a fun way to learn something new.
