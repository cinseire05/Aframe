Community Norms

Community norms​ ​are ​rules​ ​and regulations​ that are made up by a group of people that relate in a particular way. These ​rules​ ​are expected to be followed by every individual in that ​community​. Respect​ is a main ​community norm​ that sums up all ​community norms​ as a whole, because if you don't follow the ​norms​ your being disrespectful. Your​ ​community​ has come up with these ​norms​ to contain a positive space to work or live in ​unity​ and peace​.
