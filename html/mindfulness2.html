Quote of the day

The quote of the day is A people without the knowledge of their past history, origin and culture is like a tree without roots.This quote is saying that knowing where you came from and knowing your history is a step to grow. Marcus garvey believed that knowing your history is important, he knew his history very well and was even an orator for the Black Nationalism and Pan-Africanism movements.

Topic of the DAY

Juneteenth is the day the slave were freed on june 19, 1865. This holiday is usually celebrated with a large festival where people dance, sing and share food of all types. It is celebrated every june 19th.
