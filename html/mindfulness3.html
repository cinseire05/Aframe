Quote of The Day

“If you want to go quickly go alone. If you want to go far go together.”

This quote says that when you work alone it goes faster which means that when you work by yourself it might be faster but you don't really get anything out and it only benefits you.The second part is saying that if you work “together” you get something out of it and you can share your victory with someone and you essentially go father. The quote explains that community works better together.
